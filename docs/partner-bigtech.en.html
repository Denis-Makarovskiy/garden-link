02 · Who we are

A private medical residence on the Adriatic coast

Garden Recovery is a closed format for people who need systemic recovery of their physical, intellectual and psycho‑emotional state. An environment in which a person who has lost their footing again remembers what inner order, clarity, a taste for life and engagement in work feel like.

Our task is to help a person restore:

  • Resource
  • Cognitive resilience
  • Concentration
  • Quality of sleep
  • The ability to function in an intensive environment

We combine:

  • Medical supervision
  • Psychological support
  • Physiotherapy
  • Nutrition science

This is not a retreat or a form of wellness tourism. It is a managed recovery environment, created for those for whom regaining resource matters.
