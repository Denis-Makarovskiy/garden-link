01 · Point of entry

When the patient needs more than an appointment

There are conditions in which the outpatient format is no longer enough. The patient keeps functioning, but loses resource:

  • Lives in chronic tension
  • Does not follow recommendations
  • Loses control over routine, sleep and nutrition
  • Is emotionally exhausted, but not ready to seek psychiatric help
  • Is not recovering after surgery

It is at this moment that the physician needs an environment in which recovery becomes a managed process.

Garden Recovery is a private medical residence that complements the doctor's work — it does not replace it.

02 · Who we are

A private medical residence on the Adriatic coast

Garden Recovery is a closed‑format residence for patients who need systemic recovery of their physical and psycho‑emotional state.

We create a favorable environment for intensive recovery under the supervision of a team of specialists.

Our task is to help the patient stabilize their state, restore resource and return to life with greater resilience and a stronger inner foundation.

03 · Philosophy of recovery

Patients rarely state the problem directly

They do not say: "I have emotional exhaustion," "I need help" or "I am dependent."

They say:

  • "I need to pull myself together before the deal"
  • "I can't recover after the operation"
  • "I can't carry the load"
  • "I want to function normally again"

Behind these words there is often: chronic stress, sleep disturbances, exhaustion of the nervous system, problems with self‑regulation, prolonged overstrain, and the misuse of psychoactive substances, alcohol and other mood modifiers.

Garden Recovery helps build systemic work with such conditions, providing medical accompaniment while preserving the patient's quality of life.

04 · The residence

The space shapes the state

Garden Recovery is located in the Sveti Stefan area of Montenegro. A quiet, private atmosphere, the sea, the climate and the absence of external pressure all become part of the recovery process.

We create a space where:

  • The level of external tension decreases
  • Sleep and the nervous system are restored
  • The patient is able to follow the protocol
  • Sustainable recovery becomes possible

Privacy, aesthetics and respect for personal boundaries are an essential part of the Garden Recovery environment.

08 · Privacy & safety

Discretion is part of the medical approach

All medical interventions and prescriptions are carried out only after a personal consultation with a physician and on the basis of the principles of evidence‑based medicine.

Each program is formed individually, taking into account the patient's state, history, limitations and current treatment.

We work strictly within medical ethics, maintain confidentiality and do not interfere with the patient's primary treatment.

Where necessary, we:

  • Maintain communication with the referring physician
  • Coordinate limitations and recommendations
  • Provide feedback within the bounds of the patient's consent

This reduces reputational risk, helps preserve continuity of care and creates a safe environment for recovery.

09 · Partnership format

Simple and transparent collaboration

You refer the client for a preliminary and an initial consultation.

We:

  • Form a recovery program

Garden Recovery remains a complementary tool within the system of patient care — not a replacement for it.
